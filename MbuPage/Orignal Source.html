32 YEARS OF UNWAVERING TRUST,
1 UNIVERSE OF POSSIBILITIES

From a young village boy to a teacher, actor, producer, writer, Member of Parliament, philanthropist, and an educationist it has been three decades of serving my people. Yet, every moment of this enchanting journey remains fresh in my mind.

At heart, I am still that dreamer from Modhugulapalem who followed his father’s footsteps to become an educator. A proud son of a dedicated school headmaster, it humbles me to have continued his legacy, first as a teacher myself and then as the Chairman of the renowned Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Trust (SVET). Since its inception in 1992, the seed of SVET has flourished into a large Kalpavriksha helping thousands of young learners fulfill their aspirations.

Our Collaboration

Global Academic Collaborations

Mohan Babu University has established collaborations with renowned institutions worldwide, including Penn State University(USA), University of Wisconsin (USA), RWTH Aachen (Germany), Wayne State University (USA), Virginia Commonwealth University (USA), University of Pitesti (Romania), and Asia University (Taiwan). These collaborations facilitate student exchange and study abroad programs, enhancing the educational experience for our students.

Placement Partners

In the year 2022-23, the Career Development Centre engaged with over 110+ companies on campus for placements. The highest offer was 60 lakhs per annum by Google, followed by Amazons offer of 44 lakhs per annum.
